üï∏Ô∏è GNN Graph Structure: Construct a heterogeneous graph with two node types ‚Äî Player and Team. Add edges for: (1) Player‚ÜîPlayer ‚Äî shared seasons as teammates; (2) Player‚ÜíTeam ‚Äî player was auctioned to this team; (3) Player‚ÜîPlayer ‚Äî same nationality / bowling type (homophily edges). Message passing aggregates neighborhood signals so a player's auction price is influenced by the prices of similar or co-occurring players.
